Brand Story

Simply Charming is the name of a website selling jewelry with the same brand name - Simply Charming. The website sells these goods strictly within Bermuda.

The commercial success of this website was limited to the territory of Bermuda. At the same time, it is already a fairly well-known local brand. Before the launch of the website, the company sold its goods mainly through a network of tourist kiosks and stores on the islands. After the launch of the website, many locals were able to see the full list of the items available, and now easily purchase them online.

Main Challenges

  • Getting professional advice on which platform to choose from, so that it would allow our client to remain as independent as possible after the project has been completed.
  • Stringent administrative panel for the system in order to have a flexible approach to product presentation on the website: description, photos, characteristics, catalog work, etc.

The main feature of this project was that the client was limited by the budget, but at the same time they wanted to get a high-quality result based on a strong platform with a large set of basic functionality. In order to implement all the desired functional elements to the website we integrated ready-made solutions in the form of tested paid modules, which allowed us to significantly reduce the time and budget of the project. And we used Meticulosity Facepayment - a payment system that was difficult to test.

Goal

We set a goal to find a flexible system for a jewelry eStore, give clients a possibility of further support and provide wide visibility and functionality. And we needed to ensure client independence from one development and consulting partner.

Outcome

We helped develop a product that created a new sales channel for the client based on a modern and flexible tool. The client received a new tool for communication with the customer and PR of their products.
