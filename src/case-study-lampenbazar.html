Brand Story

Lampen Bazar Is a lighting online shop based in Germany. With a mission of presenting light in all its diversity for over 40 years.

Among Italian decorative lighting manufacturers, there are numerous small lamp manufacturers who have a long tradition of producing unique hand-blown glasses from Murano glass and are also leaders in hand-blown blacksmithing.

Challenges

  • Make a custom Pdf to send an invoice to customers, Scaleox developers wrote an additional module.
  • Magento-1 to Magento-2 Migration using Magento migration tool.

Goal

Develop eStore in a short time, based on a well-known open source platform.

Outcome

A new online store was developed for the customer, on a modern and flexible platform, which allowed him to communicate with consumers at a qualitatively new level, and not to be afraid to invest in advertising and increase the product range.
